This is my space to talk about my experiences with sleep so far in life. I feel I don't get enough sleep and when I do get enough sleep, I can't seem to function and I take more naps than I should, but who doesn't enjoy sleeping. It's the one thing that the humans can do right. It's our time to escape and turn off life for awhile and I think that is pretty neat!

I can't explain how much I enjoy sleeping. It's one of my favorite activities in the world. Cozy cozy cozy.

The only weird thing that I don't get about my sleeping pattern is that I don't have dreams like people do. I hear that they have them frequently, but that's never the case with me.

I'm the type of person who enjoys sleeping in the cold, as long as I have plenty of blankets on hand. I guess its the feeling of defeating the cold that I find satisfying.

Another fact about my sleeping arrangement is that I have to hear sounds while trying to fall asleep or I can't go to sleep. I've always found that very odd.

I actually wrote this code in my sleep. Only dream I've ever had. Crazy, right?
